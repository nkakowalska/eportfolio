Little about Me

Last year I finished my bachelors degree in the feild of Oceanography back in Poland, where I'm originally from. My main focuse now is gaining a lot of practical knowleage connected to remote sensing and big data as well as expanding my programming experties as I'm going to continue my CDE jurney at the GeoDataScience track at the University of Southern Brittany in Frnace next academic year.
