About Me

I'm Weronika Kowalska.

In July 2025, I completed my bachelor’s degree in Oceanography in Poland, where I am originally from. I am currently a student at the Paris Lodron University of Salzburg, enrolled in the first year of the Erasmus Mundus Joint Master (EMJM) Copernicus Master in Digital Earth (CDE). My current focus is on developing strong practical skills in remote sensing, big data processing, and programming. These competencies will be essential as I continue the CDE program next academic year in the GeoDataScience track at the University of Southern Brittany in France.

My interests center around environmental issues such as climate change, marine pollution (plastic pollution in particular) and disaster and coastal management.
